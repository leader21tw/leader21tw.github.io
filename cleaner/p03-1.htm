The Principles of Ultrasonic cleaning:

Ultrasonic cleaning as by using the high frequency and high voltage applied to the Piezoelectric transducer converting the electrical energy to mechanical energy (vibration)to the detergent in the washing tank, in which the vibration generates a lot of micro bobbles. When the bobbles rapidly achieve certain sonic pressure, there will be an explosive cavitations action. Due to the impulse of this explosive force together with proper detergent, the greasy stains, finger prints, dusts, crumbs and flux can be softened, penetrated, and desquamated, and to be cleaned completely. It works economically and efficiency for the cleaning industries.

The ultrasonic cleaning machine is especially suitable for those articles which have Complete shapes with deep holes, and those are difficult to be cleaned. In a few Minutes, you will find the incredible cleaning result.

Application:

To wash jewels, necklaces, bracelets, antiques, needle pens, paint pens, chops, Brushers, denture, razor blades, optical glasses, precision parts, nozzles, mechanical Parts, electronics parts, and dirt in finger nails etc.
