  • The wash process:
    Each building block/module is designed as an independent system. Each chamber
    is equipped with a hood. The hood rise with its weight on the sealing and hermetically separate the different chambers from each other thus barring any aerosols or fluids from escaping. Each chamber runs its specific operation independently as activated by the rack coding system. At the end of the chambers process the load is automatically moved on to the next module.
